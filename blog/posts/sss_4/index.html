SSS #4 - Development report 05/2021 - 07/2021

Hey guys, long time no see. I’ll start off by letting you know how I’ve been doing the last few months.

  • Changed my machine from a Macbook Pro 2015 to M1 Mac mini
  • Trying to make graphics with pen and paper
  • Found out that my mom has colon cancer

That’s roughly it. My mother’s surgery went well and she is now in rehab. It’s a pity that I can’t visit her due to the COVID-19. I know not many people read this blog, but I would appreciate it if you could pray for her well-being.

Now, on to the game dev stuff.
I’ve posted some of my watercolors on twitter, but the truth is, I haven’t done any pixel art for a few months now. At first, I was going to draw concept art and sketches on paper and try to incorporate them into the game as pixel art, but I’ve grown quite fond of the combination of paper, pen, and ink. Transparent watercolor is a thrilling and challenging medium, and I was completely satisfied with the results. So, with apologies to all the pixel art fans out there, I’ll probably be doing a lot less pixel art in the future.

So, I won’t be updating this devlog much (writing English is a tough job for me), and it’s hard to upload sketches since most of the people who follow me on twitter are pixel art fans… … so I created two new contents.

  • DAILY SKETCHES … I’ll upload my daily sketches here.
  • LOG(Japanese ONLY) … This is where I’ll write about my daily life and development status.

I’ll update this DEVLOG when I have some idea of what’s going on with the game. twitter is … Actually, I’m not very good at social media, so I’m not sure how I’ll use it. I don’t have anything to post except for blog updates. If I can get a cool screenshot or video, I might post it.

It’s been a few months since I got the first big response, but to be honest, things haven’t been going too well. After all, it’s a one-man studio! I hope you’ll take it easy on me. Of course, I appreciate the support. A big thank you to those of you who pay attention to the less active …… and less diligent me. If you’re interested in the development status, check out the “DAILY SKETCHES” and “LOG” above.

See you soon!
